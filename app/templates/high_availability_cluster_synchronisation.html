Configuration synchronisation

Keep both Pi-holes configured the same

Kaya compares your two Pi-holes and can copy approved settings from the main node to the standby node. Nothing is changed until you review a plan and press the final synchronise button.

    Create a planKaya reads both Pi-holes. This is safe and makes no changes.

  3. 3
    Back up and applyKaya backs up the standby, copies the settings, then checks the result.

What happens if Kaya is offline?

Your Pi-holes, DNS, DHCP and Keepalived continue running locally. Kaya coordinates and records changes, but it is not required for devices to access the network.
